Why is housing so expensive?

The current housing market is designed to extract wealth, not build stability. Federra is a democratic network designed to protect you.

By decoupling the home from the land, we’ve created a way for you to build genuine equity without the mountain of debt.

How it works.

A repeatable platform for portable housing equity.

01

Standardised Modular Homes

Residents own a portable 40-foot high-cube container home. By using the global shipping container standard, Federra leverages an established manufacturing and logistics ecosystem.

02

Shared Infrastructure Grid

Federra sites provide the essential backbone, connecting every modular home safely to power, water, sewer, and high-speed internet via standard connections.

03

Mutual Land Ownership

The land is owned collectively through a member-owned mutual. Residents pay a recurring fee to cover operational costs, preventing speculation and stabilising long-term housing costs.

True Portability

Because homes are modular, residents can relocate their container home between compatible Federra sites or upgrade without losing housing equity. Even existing container homes that pass safety inspections can move into Federra sites immediately.

The Mutual

A land-lease platform where you pay a recurring site fee to access the land and shared infrastructure. This ensures site operational costs are met while granting you membership in a community built for long-term resilience.

The Infrastructure

Pay a one-time connection fee to plug your unit into the Federra infrastructure grid. Every site operates with highly standardised lot allocation for water, sewer, power, and internet, making community deployment scalable.

The Ecosystem

Our vision includes a marketplace for compatible units and a certification program for manufacturers. Buy, sell, and relocate certified homes across our future network of interoperable sites.
